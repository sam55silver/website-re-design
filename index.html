Welcome to the Antigonish Farmers' Market

The Antigonish Farmers Market is a non-profit organization representing our community since 1993. We proudly hold 50+ vendors every week - year-round

With your help, we can enhance the local food movement and the regional economy. In our new location, the AFM is able to continue thriving and serving our community in many unique and exciting ways. The members, vendors, volunteers, and wider community of Antigonish and the surrounding areas thank you for generous support.
